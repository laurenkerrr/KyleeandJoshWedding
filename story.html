Our Story

From high school hallways to happily ever after.

It Started With a Locker and a Smile

We met in high school, trading quick smiles in the hallways and sitting a little too close at football games. Group projects turned into inside jokes, and before long, everyone else knew we were a “thing” long before we officially were.

By the time prom rolled around, it already felt like we had grown up together—through wins, losses, late-night study sessions, and way too many Chick-fil-A runs.

From Teenagers to Teammates

After graduation, life sped up. Classes, jobs, and new cities meant new challenges, but we never stopped being on the same team. We learned how to cheer each other on from the bleachers and from different zip codes.

Beach trips, concerts, and family vacations became our reset button—little reminders that no matter how busy life gets, everything feels lighter when we’re together.

Forever Started on the Sand

On a beach trip that felt like any other, Kylee thought they were just taking more photos by the water. Josh had other plans.

With the waves in the background and sun starting to set, he got a little quieter, a lot more nervous, and asked the easiest question Kylee has ever answered. She said yes—through happy tears—and the rest of the night was a blur of calls, hugs, and “finally!” from family and friends.

Now we’re counting down the days until we say “I do” in front of the people who’ve been with us since the very beginning.
